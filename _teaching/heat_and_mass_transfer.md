Teaching assistant for the **Heat and Mass Transfer** master's course at the University of Twente (2023–2025).

## Responsibilities

- Led weekly problem-solving sessions, guiding students through analytical and numerical approaches to heat conduction, convection, and mass transport problems.
- Graded assignments and provided written feedback to a cohort of ~50 students.
- Held office hours to support students with course material and exam preparation.

## Topics Covered

The course covers the fundamental governing equations and solution methods for:

- Conductive, convective, and radiative heat transfer
- Species diffusion and convective mass transport
- Dimensional analysis and similarity solutions
- Numerical approaches to transport problems
